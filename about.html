Mission & Vision

Our goal is to clearly communicate the message of hope and eternal salvation found only in Jesus Christ. This hope and eternal salvation is not found in religion or organization, but only in a living, personal and dynamic relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our mission at NLCF is to know Christ and to make Him Known.
Our vision is transforming lives and impacting the world for Jesus Christ by becoming a spiritually functioning, divinely dependent, and praying Christian community.

Leadership Team

"Pastor Louie Santelices, " Senior Pastor

Louie Santelices, Sr. Pastor of New Life in Christ Fellowship (Twin Cities) received his Master of Divinity (M.Div.) degree at Bethel Seminary/University St. Paul Minnesota, practiced his Mechanical Engineering degree and taught at De La Salle University Manila, Philippines before his call to ministry.

He served an Associate Pastor of First Filipino Baptist Church of Toronto, Ontario Canada (June 2010 – Mach 2014) and Pastor of Filipino American Christian Church (Twin Cities) (2003-2010). He also served as interim pastor, teacher, and other various capacities in several churches and organizations in the Philippines, Vancouver (BC), Montreal (PQ) and Toronto (ON), Canada.
